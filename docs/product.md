# Product Overview

A companion web app for **Sorare fantasy football players** preparing for FIFA World Cup 2026. Bridges official tournament data with Sorare card-game metrics so players can make smarter squad-building and card investment decisions across all 48 qualified nations.

## Who it's for

Sorare players who want to scout, track, and evaluate national team players ahead of WC 2026.

## Features

**World Cup Squads** — Browse any of the 48 WC 2026 national teams. Squad is shown by position (GK / Defence / Midfield / Offence) and enriched with live Sorare stats: 15-game average score and a sparkline of the last 5 SO5 performances. Users can manually add or remove players; manual changes survive re-syncs.

**Fixtures** — Full WC 2026 match schedule (via football-data.org) organized by round, with live scores, results, kickoff times, and group labels. Clicking any team opens a side panel with that team's squad and Sorare stats inline.

**Players** — Ranked, searchable list of the most popular Sorare players. Filter by position (GK / DEF / MID / FWD), search by name or club. Shows form sparkbars and average scores at a glance.

## Key differentiator

Unlike browsing Sorare directly, this app combines tournament context (fixtures, groups, rounds) with fantasy performance data (SO5 scores) in one place — purpose-built for WC 2026 decision-making.
